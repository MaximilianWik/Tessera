Round-trip verification

The QR is decoded back to text by every available decoder. Downloads stay disabled until every decoder agrees.

    Damage tolerance

    A square blot covering 5–30% of the QR's module area. Models real-world clustered damage (scratch, fade, cover-up). Bar is 5%; larger QRs typically handle much more.
